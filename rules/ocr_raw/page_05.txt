During your turn you can make up to one move and perform up to one action (Attack or
Special Action). It does not need to be the same figure that moves and performs the
action. You can't move a figure after you have performed an action.
Figures can move any number of spaces up to its Move Value. Figures move
horizontally and/or vertically. Figures Attack horizontally or vertically in a straight
line up to its Reach Value. Figures cannot occupy, move or attack through terrain or a
space that is occupied by another figure. Special Actions are not blocked by other
figures, neither yours nor your opponents, unless otherwise specified.
