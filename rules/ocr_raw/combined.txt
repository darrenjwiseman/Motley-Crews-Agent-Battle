Motley Crews is a grid-based, tactical, fantasy tabletop game for two players, that can be
played with whatever miniatures, figures or tokens you like! All you need is five figures per
player, a chessboard (or similar 8x8 grid), the cards and the rules.
My goal when coming up with the game was to make something that is easy to learn, quick
to set up and fun to play. This zine will tell you everything you need to know to get started,
and more!
For as long as anyone can remember, half the land of Loot has been under the control of the evil
Mad Mage. Now he is gone and his dark keep has crumbled to the ground. The inhabitants of Loot
are swarming to the Mages domain to reclaim their land and get a share of his treasure.

space that is occupied by another figure. Special Actions are not blocked by other

rm about the Hobby —V ©
At the heart of Motley Crews is the love of creativity- crafting,
sculpting, painting, drawing, designing, and the many other
aspects that are part of the hobby. If the game can inspire
people to pick up a paintbrush or sculpting tool-
then it’s a success!
Now, I have made “official” miniatures... But you don’t need
them to play the game! You can make your own miniatures
from scratch. You can kitbash them. You can use miniatures
from other games. You can draw your own characters and
make paper-minis! The whole reason I made the game in the
first place was so that I could play with the weird and
wonderful minis my friends had made. If you have miniatures
you love but never get to use I hope this game can give you a
reason to dust them off and play with them.
However you do it, my hope is that you will make the Motley
Crews you play something you truly enjoy, and that means you
have the freedom to change the board, minis, and even the
rules to whatever will make your personal tabletop adventure
the best it can be!
